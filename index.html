Tone‑Colored Chinese

A simple demo using your tone colors: 1st = yellow, 2nd = green, 3rd = brown, 4th = red, neutral = normal.

1st tone 2nd tone 3rd tone 4th tone neutral

我喜欢 妈妈。 (wǒ xǐhuān māma.)

你好吗？ (nǐ hǎo ma?)

今天我喝水。 (jīntiān wǒ hē shuǐ.)

How to mark tones

Wrap each character in a <span> with a tone class:

<span class="t1">妈</span>  ← 1st
<span class="t2">民</span>  ← 2nd
<span class="t3">我</span>  ← 3rd
<span class="t4">是</span>  ← 4th
<span class="tn">吗</span>  ← neutral
